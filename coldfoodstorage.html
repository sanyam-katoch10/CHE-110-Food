Refrigerator Storage

The following chart provides guidelines for storing various types of food in the refrigerator:

Food Item Refrigerator Temperature Storage Duration
Milk Below 40°F (4°C) 5-7 days
Raw Poultry Below 40°F (4°C) 1-2 days
Raw Ground Meat Below 40°F (4°C) 1-2 days
Fresh Fish Below 32°F (0°C) 1-2 days
Fruits and Vegetables Below 40°F (4°C) Varies by type
Cheese (hard) Below 40°F (4°C) 3-4 weeks
Leftovers Below 40°F (4°C) 3-4 days
Yogurt Below 40°F (4°C) 2 weeks
Butter Below 40°F (4°C) 1-3 months
Eggs Below 40°F (4°C) 3-5 weeks
Delicate Greens (lettuce, spinach) Below 40°F (4°C) 3-7 days
Apples Below 40°F (4°C) 3-4 weeks
